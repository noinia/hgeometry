allNexts :: Zipper a -> [Zipper a] Source #

Computes all nexts, even one that has no elements initially or at the end.

>>> mapM_ print $ allNexts $ fromList [1..5]
Zipper [] [1,2,3,4,5]
Zipper [1] [2,3,4,5]
Zipper [2,1] [3,4,5]
Zipper [3,2,1] [4,5]
Zipper [4,3,2,1] [5]
Zipper [5,4,3,2,1] []
