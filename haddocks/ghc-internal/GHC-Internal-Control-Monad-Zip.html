Instances should satisfy the laws:

Naturality
liftM (f *** g) (mzip ma mb)
    = mzip (liftM f ma) (liftM g mb)
Information Preservation

liftM (const ()) ma = liftM (const ()) mb implies munzip (mzip ma mb) = (ma, mb)
